Message Of The Day

  • Teaching and Learning continues in Sakai and has begun in Brightspace!

    Instructors, you should see your course shells when you log into Brightspace and all that’s left to do is add your content and make them active. Go to Brightspace here: https://brightspace.brocku.ca 

    Students, you can continue to access all of your courses through https://lms.brocku.ca.  You may have courses in Sakai and Brightspace but each course site in Sakai will identify where you can access your course materials.  Or you can choose to go directly to Brightspace for those courses that you know are being taught on that platform: https://brightspace.brocku.ca.

    Be in the Know! Opportunities for all Brock Students

    There is so much happening at Brock despite Covid-19. The best way to find out what’s being offered by faculties, services, and clubs is to explore Experience BU — Brock’s involvement portal and events calendar https://experiencebu.brocku.ca/.
